Seat
Important
Bold reference numbers in this section identify parts not shown in the figures alongside the text, but which can be found in the exploded view diagram.
Removing the seat
Passenger Seat / Seat Cover Removal
Insert key inside seat lock (22), and turn it clockwise until hook clicks.
To slide passenger seat (16) out of front retainers, push it forward, lift it, and remove it.
Rider Seat Removal
Lift rider seat edges (1), close to bracket, and loosen the retaining screws (5).
To remove rider seat (1) from vehicle, slightly pull it back, and lift it.
Side Body Panel and Tail Guard Removal
Side Body Panel Removal
To remove side body panels, rider seat shall be removed first (refer to the above procedure).
Undo the screws (6) and (7) securing RH (31) and LH (30) side body panels to rear subframe.
Remove the right (31) and left (30) side panels by sliding them from the rear tail guard.
Note
Release the lock cable end fitting (A) from its seat on LH side body panel (30).
Tail Guard Removal
To remove the tail guard, seats and side body panels shall be removed first (refer to the above procedure).
Undo screws (13), disconnect the data acquisition wiring connector (B), and remove the underseat compartment (9).
Disconnect the traction control (DTC) control unit wiring (19) connector (C).
To remove the traction control (DTC) control unit (19), loosen nuts (29).
Disconnect tail light wiring connector (D).
Remove number plate holder (Sect. H 7, Removing the number plate holder).
Undo the screws (7), and remove the lower tail guard (17).
To remove the lower tail guard (17), slide out side tabs (E) from their slots (F) on upper tail guard (12).
Loosen upper tail guard (12) retaining screws (21) and (27), and remove tail guard from subframe.
Removing the seat release mechanism
Seat opening device (22) is positioned inside LH side body panel (30).
Remove LH side body panel (30), as described in the previous paragraph.
Release cable (20) from plate (18), and seat cable end fitting (A) from its seat on lock.
Loosen nut (14), and remove plate (18) and seat lock (22).
Refitting the seat release mechanism
Insert lock (22) inside the special hole on LH side body panel (2), aiming it with the "OPEN" wording pointing up, as shown in the figure.
Insert plate (18) on side body panel inner side, aiming it as shown in the figure (fixed position).
Secure lock (22) and plate in place starting nut (14) into its thread.
Tighten nut (14) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Insert lock cable (20) end fitting (A) inside its seat on lock (20).
Secure lock cable (20) in place installing it on plate (18), as shown in the figure.
Side Body Panel and Tail Guard Reassembly
Tail Guard Reassembly
Make sure that quick-release fasteners (11) and (28) are present on rear subframe, and that quick-release fasteners (11) are present on upper tail guard (12).
If the traction control unit (DTC) (19) has been removed, screw spacers (24) all the way down, position spring washers (25) and install traction control unit (DTC) (19), aiming it as shown in the figure.
Fit and tighten nuts (29) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Connect the connector (G) coming from the rear wiring branch to the tail guard connector (D), as shown in the figure.
Position upper tail guard (12) onto subframe, and secure it in place starting rear special screws (21) and front special screws (27) into their threads.
Tighten the screws (21) and (27) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Fit lower tail guard (17) sliding number plate light and turn indicator wiring (H) inside hole, and inserting side tabs (E) inside the special slots (F).
Secure lower tail guard (17) with screws (7) tightened to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Refit number plate holder (Sect. H 7, Refitting the number plate holder).
Connect tail light wiring connector (D).
Connect traction control (DTC) control unit wiring (19) connector (C).
Position underseat compartment (9) onto tail guard, connect data acquisition wiring connector (B), and secure in place starting screws (13) into their threads. Tighten the screws (13) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Refit seats and side body panels, as described in the above procedure.
Side Body Panel Reassembly
Make sure that quick-release fasteners (4) are present on RH (31) and LH (30) side body panel tabs (B), aimed as shown in the figure.
Draw LH side body panel (30) up to tail guard, and insert lock cable end fitting (A) inside the special seat on lock (20).
Secure cable in place installing it on plate (18), as shown in the figure.
Position RH (31) and LH (30) side body panels inserting tab (B) under tail guard, as shown in the figure.
Tighten RH body panel (2) by inserting screws (7) and (6).
Tighten the screws (6) and (7) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Refitting the seat
Passenger Seat / Seat Cover Reassembly
Insert the passenger seat (16) bottom tabs inside the special openings on tail guard, pressing them under frame tube.
Push passenger seat (16) rear side until you hear the pin clicking inside latch.
To make sure that tabs are correctly hooked, gently pull passenger seat up.
Passenger Seat Reassembly
Position seat (1) inserting tabs (A) inside the special tank notches (B), as shown in the figure.
Lift seat edges (1), close to the brackets, and start retaining screws (5) into their threads.
Tighten the screws (5) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
