Adjusting the throttle cable
To operate on the throttle cable adjusters, consult Sect. D 4, Adjusting the throttle cable.
Removing the throttle twistgrip
Remove the plug (3) from the handlebar.
Slightly pull back the rubber sleeve (A) protecting the throttle control cables.
Undo the screws (B) of the throttle grip (5) and open the control.
Remove the throttle grip cables (2) by unhooking the cable ends (C) from their seats.
Remove the throttle twistgrip (5) from the handlebar.
Note
To replace the throttle grip cables, it is necessary to remove them from the throttle body (Sect. L 6, Removing the airbox and throttle body).
Spare throttle cables are supplied complete with adjusters.
 
Refitting the throttle twistgrip
Lubricate the ends of the throttle cables (2) and the twistgrip race with the recommended grease.
To refit the throttle control components, proceed in inverse order to the removal procedure, making sure that the twistgrip housings locate in the reference hole in the handlebar.
