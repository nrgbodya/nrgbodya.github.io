Loosen the clamp (5) and remove the hose (6) from the fuel filler (11).
Loosen the two screws (8) securing the bracket to the (4) intake manifold.
Remove the tank (1) with bracket (4) and hoses (6) and (7).

Secure the hoses (6) and (7) to the tank using the clamps (5).
Tighten the screws (3) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values) with washers (9) to secure tank (1) to bracket (4).
Fit the tank (1) with the bracket (4) and the hoses (6) and (7).
Start the two screws (8) of the bracket (4), and tighten them to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Position the hose (6) on the fuel filler (11) with the clamp (5).

Loosen the screws (B) and remove the protection (A).
Loosen the fixing clamps (20) of the lower water radiator (C) hose (18) to the pump and the fixing clamps of the radiator/thermostat sleeve (29) to the water upper radiator (D).
Loosen the clamp (25) fixing the radiator/thermostat sleeve (29) to the heat exchanger (E).
Loosen the clamp (21) fixing the radiator/thermostat sleeve (29) to the horizontal cylinder head intake manifold (F).
Loosen the clamp (25) fixing the rubber sleeve (26) to the heat exchanger (E).
 
Loosen the clamps (24) securing the thermostat assembly (19) thermostat-cylinder head sleeve (17) thermostat-cylinder head sleeve (16) and sleeve (29).
Loosen the clamps (20) and (24) to disassemble the thermostat unit (19) - thermostat/cylinder head sleeve (17) - thermostat/cylinder head sleeve (16) and sleeve (29).
Loosen the clamp (25) and remove the rubber sleeve (26) from the union (27).
Loosen the clamp (5) and remove the radiator/plug sleeve (14) from the union (30).
Loosen the screws (15) and remove union (30).
Recover the O-ring (23) located between the retainer and the vertical head.
Disconnect the temperature sensor connector (G), positioned on the horizontal cylinder head union (28), from the main wiring.
Undo the screws (15) and remove the horizontal cylinder union (28).
Collect the O-ring (23) located between the union and the horizontal head.
Important
Periodically check the connection unions for leaks.
Hoses that are cracked, swollen, or hardened should be replaced.
Refitting the cooling system hoses and unions
Install an O-ring (23) in the seat of the horizontal head union (28) and one in union seat (30).
Fix the union (28) on the horizontal cylinder head with the screws (15) and tighten them to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Fix the union (30) on the vertical cylinder head with the screws (15) and tighten them to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Connect the temperature sensor connector (G), positioned on the horizontal cylinder head union (28), to the main wiring.
Fit the radiator/plug sleeve (14) on the union (30), then tighten the clamp (5).
Position the rubber sleeve (26) on the union (27), then tighten the clamp (25).
To assemble the thermostat unit (19) - thermostat/cylinder head sleeve (17) - thermostat/cylinder head sleeve (16) - sleeve (29), position the clamps (20) and (24) and tighten them to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Position the thermostat unit (19) - thermostat/cylinder head sleeve (17) - thermostat/cylinder head sleeve (16) - sleeve (29) with the clamps (24), and tighten them to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Position the radiator/thermostat sleeve (29) on the heat exchanger (E).
Position the radiator/thermostat sleeve (29) on the horizontal cylinder head intake manifold (F).
Position the rubber sleeve (26) on the heat exchanger (E).
Tighten the clamps (21) and (25) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Position the pump/radiator hose (18) and the radiator/thermostat sleeve (29) with the clamps (20) and tighten the clamps to the specified torque (Sect. 3 - , Frame tightening torque values).
Position the protection (A), and tighten the screws (B) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
