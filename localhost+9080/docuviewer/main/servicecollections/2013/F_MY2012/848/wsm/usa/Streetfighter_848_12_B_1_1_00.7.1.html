Streetfighter 848 identification data
Each Ducati motorcycle has two identification numbers -the frame number and the engine number- and an EC nameplate (A) (not present on the U.S.A. version).
Note
Please quote these numbers, which identify the motorcycle model, when ordering spare parts.
