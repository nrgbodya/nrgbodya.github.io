Disconnect the injectors (13) of the main wiring.
Remove the fixing clips (A).
Loosen the clamps (1) securing the throttle body to the intake manifolds (B).
Disconnect the connector (C) connecting the main wiring loom to the stepper motor (33).
Disconnect the connector (D) connecting the main wiring harness to the potentiometer (E).
Loosen the clamp (21) that retains the hose (18) with the blow-by valve.
Remove the airbox (20) together with the throttle body (11) and the injectors (13).
Removing the fuel injectors
Undo the screws (3), and remove the injectors (13) with hoses (14) and (16) from the airbox.
Refitting the injectors
Ensure that the injector O-rings (2) are present.
Position the hose and union (5), (6), (14), (15) and (16) assembly with the injectors (13) on the airbox (20) in the special yellow grooves (F).
Apply the recommended threadlocker to the screws (3).
Tighten the screws (3) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Removing the stepper motor
The stepper motor is installed in the central part of the throttle body:
to remove it, loosen the two screws (G), disconnect the hoses (H) and (31) from the throttle body and from the airbox. Refer to Sect. M 3, Stepper motor for details on its operation.
Refitting the stepper motor
Fit the hoses (H) on the throttle body, and the hose (31) on the airbox pressure test fitting (30). To fit the stepper motor hose (31), insert the "short" side inside the throttle body (11) union (L), and the "long" side inside the nipple previously assembled on the airbox (20). Locate the motor with its support on the throttle body, fitting the screws (G).
Tighten the screws (G) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Disassembling airbox – throttle body
Undo the six screws (8). Remove the hose (31) from the pressure test fitting (30). Remove the throttle body (11) from the airbox (20).
 
Reassembling airbox – throttle body
If removed, fit the sealing washers (9) in the seat (M) of the airbox (20).
If removed, start and tighten the nipple (30) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Bind the fuel hose (5) coming from the injector assembly to the airbox (20) using a small clamp, as shown in the figure.
To fit the fuel hoses (N), install them inside the union (5).
Secure the hoses (N) with the clamps (27), aiming them as shown in the figure.
Install the hose grommet (23) to the airbox, aiming it as shown in the figure. Apply recommended threadlocker on the screws (24), and start the screws (24) into their threads, placing the washers (22) between the hose grommet and the airbox.
Tighten the screws (24) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
If removed, insert the sealing washers (10) in the seats of the intake funnels.
If removed, refit the funnels (7) on the throttle body (11), apply recommended threadlocker to the screws (12), and tighten to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values) following the indicated sequence.
Position the throttle body (11) on the airbox (20), aiming it so that the pulley (O), once assembled, is close to the airbox "recess" (P), as shown in the figure.
Secure the throttle body (11) to the airbox (20), starting the screws (8).
Connect the hose (26) to the pressure test fitting (15).
Tighten the six screws (8) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values), observing the indicated sequence.
If removed, refit the throttle cables on the throttle body as follows: install the opening (P) and closing (Q) cables inside their seats on the throttle body plate, as shown in the figure.
Insert the bolts (S) in the corresponding seats of the pulley. Keep oriented as shown in figure, fasten the cables (P) and (Q) by inserting the nuts (R
). Tighten the nuts (R) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Fit the dust caps (O), driving them fully home on nuts (R).
Make sure that the throttle opening cable (P) and closing cable (Q) are correctly inserted inside tab (Z).
If removed, refit the cable grommet (23), the screw (24) with recommended threadlocker, and the two washers (22). Tighten the screw (24) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Fit the drain hose (19) by inserting it fully home on the union on the airbox (20).
Aim drain hose so that, once assembled, the notch present on the hose matches with the union tooth (T), as shown in the figure.
Secure the hose in place with the clamp (29).
Refitting airbox and throttle body
Position the clamps (1) on their seats on the intake manifolds (B).
Position the airbox (20) and the throttle body (11) on the vehicle.
Tighten the clamps (1) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Connect the connectors from the main wiring to the injectors (13), and secure them in place with the ties (A).
Connect the main wiring connector (D) to the stepper motor (33).
Connect the main wiring connector (D) to the potentiometer (E).
 
Position the hose (18) again on the blow-by valve, and tighten the clamp (21).
Important
If the throttle body is changed, after fitting the new throttle body, connect the vehicle to the Diagnosis Tester (DDS 2) to reset the TPS (throttle potentiometer).
