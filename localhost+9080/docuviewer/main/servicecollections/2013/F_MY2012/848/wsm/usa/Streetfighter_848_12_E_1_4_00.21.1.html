Removing the rear-view mirrors
Undo the screws (2), remove the rear-view mirrors (1) and recover the washers (3).
Refitting the rear-view mirrors
Start the screws (2) in the rear-view mirrors (1), inserting the washers (3) as shown in the picture.
Insert the rear-view mirrors (1) in the U-bolts (4).
Tighten the screws (2) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
 
Warning
The left rear-view mirror screw (2) has a left-handed threading.
