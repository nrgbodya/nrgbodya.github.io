Number Plate Holder
Important
Bold reference numbers in this section identify parts not shown in the figures alongside the text, but which can be found in the exploded view diagram.
Removing the number plate holder
The Australia version features the gravel guard (33) with the screws (34) and the spacers (35), within the number plate holder assembly.
Undo the screws (19) and (1).
For the Australia version, undo also the screws (34) securing the gravel guard (33) to the splash-guard (20), and keep the spacers (35).
Undo the screws (7) to remove the plate (8) from the number plate holder (6).
For The Australia version, undo the screws (7) to remove the plate (8) and the gravel guard (33) from the number plate holder (6).
Plate (8) has a different shape on the USA version.
Lift the splash-guard (20) from the number plate holder (6), taking care to the inner wiring.
Disconnect the wiring fastons (A) from the number plate light reeds, and release the splash-guard (20).
Remove the spacers (2).
Remove the clips (B) from the rear turn indicator connectors (C), and disconnect connectors (C) from main wiring connectors (D).
Loosen the screws (15).
Slide the rubber pad (18) out of the number plate holder (6), remove the support (16), and slide out the wiring with the rubber pad (18) from the number plate holder (6) so as to release it from the tail guard.
Refitting the number plate holder
Check the presence of clips (17) on the number plate holder (6).
Position the number plate holder (6) on the under tail guard.
Position the support (16) on the number plate holder.
Insert the spacers (29) on the screws (15), and secure both the number plate holder and the support with the screws (15).
Tighten the screws to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Fit the rubber pad (18) on the number plate holder, sliding the number plate light and the turning light wiring inside it.
Connect the rear turn indicator connectors (C) to the main wiring connectors (D).
Position the connector (C) clips (B).
Fit the spacers (2).
Connect the wiring fastons (A) to the number plate light reeds on the splash-guard (20).
With the utmost care, position the left turn indicator wiring and the number plate light wiring inside their seats on the number plate holder.
Bring the splash-guard (20) near the number plate holder (6).
Fit the splash guard (20) on the number plate holder (6), paying attention not to press the wires underneath.
Apply the recommended threadlocker to the screws (1).
Fix the splash-guard (20) inserting the screws (1) and (19).
Tighten the screws (1) and (19) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
For the Australia version, position the gravel guard (33) between the splash-guard (20) and the number plate holder (6), and fit the spacers with collar (35) in-between the two guards, aiming them as shown in the figure.
Fit the gravel guard (33) starting and tightening the screws (34) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Fit the plate (8) on the number plate holder (6), tightening the screws (7) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3 - , Frame tightening torque values).
For the Australia version, position plate (8) on the gravel guard (33).
