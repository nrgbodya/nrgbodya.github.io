Undo and remove the screws (3) securing the upper U-bolt (1).
Remove the upper U-bolt (1).
Remove the handlebar (4) from its seat on the steering head.
To remove the grips (5), refer to the exploded view at the beginning of this chapter.
While holding the nuts (9), undo the screws (2), and remove the lower U-bolt (8) from the steering head; recover washers (7) and (10).
 
 
Refitting the handlebar
If the spacers (6) were removed from the steering head, lubricate with silicone spray.
Seat the spacers (6) in the bores in the steering head, aiming them as shown in the figure.
Upon completion of the operation, clean all excess lubricant from the components.
Position the lower U-bolt (8) on the steering head with washers (10).
Fit the screws (2) in the U-bolt (8), screw the nuts (9) complete with washers (7).
Tighten the screws (2) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values) while holding nuts (9).
Position handlebar (4) in its seat on the lower U-bolt (8).
Apply the recommended grease to the threads and underside of the screws (3).
Refit the upper U-bolt (1) and insert the screws (3).
Tighten the screws (3) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values), applying sequence 1-2-3-4-3, as indicated.
To refit the grips (5), refer to the exploded view at the beginning of this chapter.
