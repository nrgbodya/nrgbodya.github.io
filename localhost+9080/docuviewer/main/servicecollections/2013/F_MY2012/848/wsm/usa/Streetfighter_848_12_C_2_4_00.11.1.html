Important
Do not use additives in fuel or lubricants. The use of these fuels can cause serious damage to the engine and to vehicle parts.
Warning
Vehicle is compatible only with fuels with a max. ethanol content of 10% (E10). The use of fuels with an ethanol percentage above 10% is forbidden. The use of these fuels can cause serious damage to the engine and to vehicle parts. The use of fuels with an ethanol percentage above 10% voids bike warranty.
