Important
Bold reference numbers in this section identify parts not shown in the figures alongside the text, but which can be found in the exploded view diagram.
Removing the headlight
Remove the clamp (A), and release the LH switch connector from main wiring.
Unscrew the nuts (14) securing the front light assembly to the bottom yoke.
Lift the complete headlight assembly by sliding pins (B) out of support (2).
Disconnect the following connectors (refer to the tables in chapter “Routing of wiring on frame”, Sect. P 1):
-
front wiring connector (C);
-
immobilizer antenna wiring connector (D);
-
RH switch wiring connector (E).
 
Remove the headlight from the vehicle.
Disconnect the headlight connector (F) and the air temperature sensor connector (G).
Release the main wiring connector (H) from the bracket (3).
Remove the air temperature sensor (29) and the rubber pad (30) from the sensor support (31).
Loosen the screws (6) and release the headlight (12) from the complete subframe (32); remove the support (31), the support (3), and the front wiring cable grommet (35).
Working on the rear side, release the complete subframe (32) from the tabs (L) and the cowlings (M) on the headlight fairing (10), then remove it.
Loosen the screws (34) and remove the headlight fairing (10) from the headlight (12). Recover the washer (36).
Disassembling the headlight supporting subframe
Undo the screws (25) and remove the instrument panel cover (26).
Disconnect the instrument panel connector (N), loosen the screws (19), and remove the instrument panel (22) from the complete subframe (32).
Loosen the screws (17) and separate the central headlight support (16) from the LH (15) and RH (23) supports.
Loosen the screws (7), keep the plates (8), and remove the LH (9) and RH (24) turn indicators.
Reassembling the headlight supporting subframe
Make sure that the quick-release fasteners (18) are present on the LH (15) and RH (23) headlight supports.
Fit the LH (9) and RH (24) turn indicators on the relevant supports, respecting the identification wordings (O) on cables.
Apply threadlocker to the screws (7).
Secure the LH (9) and RH (24) turn indicators in place with the screws (7), placing plates (8) in-between.
Tighten the screws (7) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
 Note
For the USA version, the RH and LH spacers (R) shall be fitted between the supports and the turn indicators, with the slots (S) facing down. Slide the turn indicator cables inside the slots, without pulling them too much so as to avoid the detachment of the faston present inside the turn indicators.
Join the central headlight support (16) to the LH (15) and RH (23) supports, starting the screws (17).
Tighten the screws (17) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Position the instrument panel (22) inside its seat, start the screws (19), and connect the instrument panel connector (N).
Tighten the screws (19) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
To fit the instrument panel cover (26), insert the profile (P) inside the groove (Q).
 
Fit and tighten the screws (25) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Reassembling the headlight
Make sure that the quick-release fasteners (18) and (33) are present on the headlight fairing (10) and on the headlight (12).
Insert the headlight (12) inside the headlight fairing (10), and secure it in place with the screws (34) and the washer (36).
Fit the headlight assembly on the complete subframe (32), making sure to correctly engage the headlight fairing (10) edge under the instrument panel cover (26).
On the back side, lock the complete subframe (32) under the tabs (L), and insert the subframe cowlings (M) inside their seats on the headlight fairing (10).
Fit the headlight (12) to the complete subframe (32) starting the screws (6).
Refit the support (31), the support (3) and the front wiring cable grommet (35).
Refit the rubber pad (30) on the support plate (31) and the air temperature sensor (29).
Insert the main wiring connector (H) on the support (3).
Connect the headlight connector (F) and the air temperature sensor connector (G).
Connect the following connectors (refer to the tables in chapter “Routing of wiring on frame”, Sect. P 1):
-
front wiring connector (C);
-
immobilizer antenna wiring connector (D);
-
RH switch wiring connector (E).
Refit the complete headlight assembly sliding pins (B) inside the support (2).
Fit the clip (A), and secure the LH switch connector to the main wiring.
Tighten the nuts (14) securing the front light assembly to the bottom yoke.
