Disconnect the exchanger pipes
N 3.1, Removing the cooling system hoses and unions
Loosen the nuts (A) of pipes (7) and (11) from nipples (5) and disconnect them from engine.
Loosen nipples (5) on the crankcase half, and collect the gaskets (4).
Upon disassembly, make sure not to lose the O-rings (B) that guarantee the coupling sealing.
Loosen and remove the pin (C) securing the heat exchanger bracket (10) to the upper water radiator.
Loosen the screws (8), and remove the bracket (10), with the assembled heat exchanger, from the horizontal cylinder head.
Loosen the screws (9) and remove the heat exchanger (1) from the bracket (10); keep the spacers (3).
Loosen the nuts (D) of pipes (7) from hoses (5), and disconnect them from the heat exchanger.
Loosen the nipples (5) on the heat exchanger, and collect the gaskets (6).
Inspecting the heat exchanger
Visually inspect the heat exchanger. Replace it at any sign of damage or leaks.
Refitting the lubrication system
Note
Before fitting the hoses (7) and (11), it is recommended to check the presence of the O-rings (B). Lubricate them by using engine oil.
Install one washer (6) on each nipple (5), and apply the recommended threadlocker on the threads on the exchanger side.
 Note
Washer (6) sharp edge shall be facing the heat exchanger.
 
Start the nipples (5) on the heat exchanger (1), then tighten them to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Engine tightening torque values) while holding the hex nuts.
Lubricate the nipples threads (5), on hose side, with engine oil.
Attach the hoses to the nipples (5) of the heat exchanger, finger-tightening the ring nuts (E).
Tighten ring nuts (E) to the specified torque (Sect. 3 - , Engine tightening torque values) counterholding the hex nuts of the nipples.
Refit the bracket (10) with the heat exchanger on the horizontal cylinder head, and tighten the screws (8) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Position the gaskets (4) and the nipples (5) inside the crankcase holes.
Tighten the nipples (5) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Engine tightening torque values).
Position oil delivery (7) and return (11) hoses inside the crankcase, and tighten the retaining nuts (A).
