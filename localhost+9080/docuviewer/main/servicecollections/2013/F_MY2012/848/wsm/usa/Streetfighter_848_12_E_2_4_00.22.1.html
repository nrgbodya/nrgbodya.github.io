Removing the cowling
To remove the RH cowling (1), loosen screws (3) and (5): recover washers (4), spacers (6), and vibration dampers (7).
Follow the same procedure to remove the LH cowling (2).
Reassembling the cowling
Position the RH cowling (1) on radiator, starting screws (3) with washers (4) and screws (5) with spacers (6) and vibration dampers (7).
Tighten the screws (3) and (5) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Follow the same procedure to refit the LH cowling (2).
