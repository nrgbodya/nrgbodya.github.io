Removing the front footpegs
The complete LH footpeg can be removed only after the gearchange pedal (A) has been removed from the plate (2) (Sect. F 5, Disassembling the gearchange control).
Loosen the two retaining screws (7) on frame, and remove the bracket with the footpeg assembly.
Keep the spacers (22).
To separate the footpeg from the holding bracket, remove the snap ring (10) and slide out the pin (4) so as to release both footpeg and spring (3).
Refitting the front footpegs
Install the footpeg (5) or (6) inside the footpeg bracket (1) or (2) respectively, with spring (3) in-between, as shown in the figure.
Insert spring end (B) inside hole (C) on footpeg bracket, and spring end (D) on footpeg surface (E).
Secure footpeg to the relevant bracket with pin (4), on the side shown in the figure.
Lock pin (4) installing circlip (10) inside the groove on pin end.
Fit the complete assembly on frame with spacers (22), secure in place with screws (7), and apply the recommended threadlocker.
Tighten the screws (7) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Refit the gearchange pedal (Sect. F 5, Refitting the gearchange mechanism) on the left footpeg bracket.
Removing of the rear footpegs
Remove the rider seat (Sect. E 3, Removing the seat).
Undo the screws (A) and remove the motor cover (B).
Loosen the screw (C) and the nut (D), and remove the silencer unit from the bracket (18) (Sect. L 8, Removing the exhaust system).
Loosen the screws (12), keep the washers (8), and remove the brackets (17) and (18).
To separate the footpeg (15) from the relevant bracket (17), remove the snap ring (14), and drive out pin (16) to release the footpegs, the spring (19), the ball (20) and the block (21).
The procedure is the same for the LH rear footpeg (13).
Refitting the rear footpegs
Insert spring (19) inside passenger LH footpeg (17) hole (E). Position ball (20) so that it rests against spring (19), footpeg block (21) and footpeg (15).
Lock footpeg (15) in place with pin (16).
Lock pin (16) installing snap ring (14) inside the groove (F) on pin.
The procedure is the same for the rear footpeg (13).
Position brackets (17) and (18), starting screws (12) with washers (8), and tighten screws (12) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Position the silencer assembly on the bracket (18) tightening the nut (D) and the screw (C) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values): refer to Sect. L 8, Refitting the exhaust system.
Refit the cover (B) and tighten the screws (A) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Refit the rider seat (Sect. E 3, Removing the seat).
