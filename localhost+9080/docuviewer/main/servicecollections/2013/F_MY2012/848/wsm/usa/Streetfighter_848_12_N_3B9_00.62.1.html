Inspecting the water radiators
Visually inspect the radiators. Replace the radiators at any sign of damage or leaks.
Check also that the air flow through the radiator fins are not obstructed by leaves, insects, mud, etc.
Important
Excessive coolant temperature can be caused by a partial obstruction of the radiator fins.
 
Carefully check the condition of the radiator fins.
Replacing the cooling fan
Undo the electric fan three retaining screws (H) and remove the electric fan (9) from the radiator.
Upon reassembly, fit and tighten the screws (H) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Reassembling the water radiators
