AIR INTAKE - OIL BREATHER
Important
Bold reference numbers in this section identify parts not shown in the figures alongside the text, but which can be found in the exploded view diagram.
Removing the air filters
Working on both sides, loosen the retaining screws (1), and keep the spacers (16).
Remove the external conveyors (7) and (10).
Loosen the screws (2) on the internal air conveyors (6) and (9), and remove them from the airbox.
Remove the air filter (5).
Refitting the air filters
Apply universal sealant in the air conveyor (6) and (9) grooves (A).
Insert the sealing washers (8) inside their slots, taking care to correctly engage the reference teeth (B) inside their seats.
Fit the sealing washers (3), aiming them as shown in the figure.
Refit the air conveyors (6) and (9).
Start the air conveyor (6) and (9) screws (2), and tighten to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
Fit the internal air conveyors (7) and (10).
Start the screws (1) inserting the spacers (16) on the upper ones.
Tighten the screws (1) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame tightening torque values).
